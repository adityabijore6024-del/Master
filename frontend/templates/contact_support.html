Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common student roadblocks.

How can I buy a course?

Open the course catalog from your dashboard, select the professional track you wish to master, and click the 'Buy Course' button to complete payment via UPI or Cards.

Payment completed but course not showing?

Occasionally bank Webhook confirmations take up to 10 minutes. If your course doesn't unlock automatically, create a 'Payment' ticket above with your UPI Transaction ID.

Can I ask AI doubts?

Yes! Our Cortex-9™ AI Virtual Professor is integrated directly into your student suite. Click the 'Ask AI' card above to launch the interactive solver.
